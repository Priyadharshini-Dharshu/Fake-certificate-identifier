What is Certificate Verification?

Our AI-powered certificate verification system uses advanced machine learning and OCR technology to analyze and verify the authenticity of certificates in real-time.

Whether you're an educational institution, employer, or organization, our system helps you quickly determine if a certificate is genuine or potentially fraudulent.

How It Works

Upload an image of the certificate you want to verify. Supported formats include PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, and BMP.

Our system analyzes multiple features including image sharpness, text density, contrast, entropy, and looks for suspicious regions.

Receive an instant authenticity verdict with confidence score and detailed feature analysis.
